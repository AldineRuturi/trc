About Us

TRC is a bible reading and believing church that believes in the trinity,thus, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit. We are strongly anchored on preaching the Truth of the gospel of Jesus Christ and through praise and worship to bring salvation to mankind in conformity to the great commission.


Our Vision

To manifest the excellence of God as His instruments effectively ministering the gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations.


Our Mission

Reaching out to lost souls, the broken-hearted and the needy with the truth of the gospel of Jesus Christ, serving them with integrity and compassion giving God His highest praises in the beauty of His holiness.
