About Us

Message from Presiding President (Senior Pastor Really Deve)

Our brethren in Christ Jesus, we are delighted and greatly excited that you are here online with us. Welcome, welcome and welcome. Please take a look around to learn more about who we are as TRC

Our Vision

Is to manifest God’s excellence as His instruments effectively ministering the gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations.

Our Mission

Is reaching out to lost souls, the broken-hearted and the needy with the truth of the gospel of Jesus Christ, serving them with integrity and compassion giving God His highest praises in the beauty of His holiness.

Our Beliefs

We believe in the Godhead: The Father, The Son and The Holy Spirit. We believe they are one and in absolute and perfect unity, yet each with particular and personal attributes.

Colossians 2 v 9; 1 John 5 v 7; John 1 v 1-4; John 10 v 30

We believe in the Godhead: The Father, The Son and The Holy Spirit. We believe they are one and in absolute and perfect unity, yet each with particular and personal attributes.

Ephesians 4 v 6; John 3 v 1.

We believe that Jesus Christ is the Only Begotten of the Father, The Anointed One, The Messiah. He is truly God and truly Man born of the Virgin Mary, lived a sinless life, died on the Cross for the Sin of the World, resurrected bodily and is seated at God’s right hand in the Heavenly places.

1 Peter 3 v 22; Ephesians 1 v 20; Luke 22 v 69; John 1 v 14; John 12 v 45; John1 v 1-2; Galatian 4 v 4; and Hebrews 1 v 4-6.

We believe that the Holy Spirit is the Spirit of God who dwells in believers.

Ezekiel 36 v 27; John 14 v 17; Romans 8 v 9.

We believe that the Word of God is:
Inspired
We believe that the written Word of God is divinely inspired, and has been given to us by God as the complete rule of faith and practice. We believe it is the foundation for Christian growth and maturity.
2 Timothy 3 v 16; Jeremiah36 v 2; Acts 1v 16; 2 Peter 1 v 20-21.
Sacred
We believe the Word of God is sacred and it needs not to be altered. We ought not to add or subtract from it.
Deuteronomy 4 v 2; Deuteronomy 12 v 32; Proverbs 30 v 6; Revelations 22 v 19.
Trustworthy
We believe the Word of God is absolutely trustworthy and not one word will fail of His promises.
1 kings 8 v 56; Psalms 111 v 7; Ezekiel 12 v 25; Mathew 5 v 18; Luke 21 v 33

We believe in the fallen nature and depravity of man, by reason of which he is unable of himself to please God apart from God’s grace through the redemption that is in Christ Jesus.

Romans 3 v 23-24; Acts 13 v 39; Romans 5 v 1; 1 Corinthians 1 v 30; Colossians 1 v 14; Titus 2 v 14

We believe confession of sin is a prerequisite to receiving the Lord’s forgiveness and cleansing; and that true repentance brings about the changing of the heart, mind, attitude and deposition in regard to sin and produces complete reformation in behavior and attitude towards God.

Hosea 14 v 2; Mathew 3 v 2; Mathew 4 v 17; Acts 3 v 19; Acts 17 v 30

We believe in the elect purpose of the Grace of God, whereby He, through the sacrificial death, resurrection and ascension of His Son, Jesus Christ, provided for man a means of justification regeneration, and sanctification, which blessings are granted upon a person’s repentance and faith in Him.

We believe that every believer has been given the assurance that he has been saved and justified and sanctified by The Blood of Jesus, and that all past sins are forgiven. We believe that the moment he calls on the name of the Lord he shall be saved.

Acts 16 v 31; Psalms 37 v 39; John 10 v 9; Acts 4 v 12; Hebrews 5 v 9; Hebrews 9 v 28.

We believe that Jesus Christ, who is The Head of the Church, through His Word and the Scriptural ministries, governs the Church, which is the Body of Christ and the fellowship of the saints.

Ephesians 1 v 22-23; Colossians 1 v 18

We believe in the following Christian Sacraments:
Water Baptism
We believe in water baptism by immersion, when man believes that Jesus Christ is Lord.
John 3 v 5; Acts 8 v 36-39
Holy Spirit Baptism
We believe in the Baptism by the Holy Spirit with evidence of speaking in tongues. We believe that the baptism in The Holy Spirit empowers believers for service, and the manifestations of His fruit, gifts and graces.
Mathew 3 v 11-15; Acts 1 v 8; Luke 24 v 49; 1 Corinthians v 4-11; 1 Corinthians 14 v 2, 4; Galatians 5 v 22
Lord’s Super
We believe that partaking the Lord Super is an instruction from the Lord and by so doing we will be remembering Him and proclaiming His death till He comes.
I Corinthians 11 v 24-26; Mathew 26 v 26-28

We believe in the Second Advent of our Lord Jesus Christ, and earnestly await Jesus Christ’s return.

Revelation 1 v 7; Luke 21 v 27; Acts 1 v 11; Hebrews 9 v 28; Luke 24 v 27-36

We believe in the bodily resurrection of mankind at the second coming/advent of Jesus Christ.
John 5 v 25; John 6 v 40; 1 Corinthians 15 v 51-58; 1 Thessalonians 4 v 14.
Eternal Judgement
We believe that we will appear before the judgement seat Christ and each one will be judged according to their works done while on this body, whether good or bad.
2 Corinthians 5 v 9-10; 1 Peter 1 v 17; Revelations 20 v 12.
Final doom of Satan
We believe that Satan who is the deceiver of nations will be bound and thrown into the lake of fire and brimstone and will be tormented day and night for ever and ever.
Revelations 20 v 10.
New heaven and new earth
We believe in the new heaven and new earth and we believe that the first heaven and the first earth will pass ways and all things will be made new.
Revelations 21 v 1; Isaiah 65 v 17; 2 Peter 3 v 10,13.

We believe that the welfare of the needy is the particular responsibility of all those who believe in God.

James 2 v 14-17; 1 John 3 v 17-18

We believe that evangelism is the ultimate goal of Christianity. We believe that it the responsibility of Believers to portray Jesus Christ to unbelievers and remove them from the Kingdom of Darkness into the Kingdom of Light.

Mathew 28 v 19; Luke 24 v 47; Colossians 1 v 23.

We believe that every Believer is no longer a stranger but a fellow citizen with the saints and members of God’s household.

Ephesians 2 v 19

The Institution of marriage
We believe the institution of marriage was instituted by God and is based upon the Word of God, joining one man and one woman to become one flesh.
Genesis 2 v 18; Genesis 2 v 24.
Polygamy
We believer in monogamy and that if a man or woman living in a polygamous union becomes a believer, he or she cannot hold a position of leadership in church and cannot marry another woman after becoming a believer.
1 Timothy 3 v 2-23; Titus 1 v 6-9.
Divorce
We believe that a husband is not to divorce his wife, and wife is not to depart from her husband.
Malachi 2 v 14-16; 1 Corinthians 7 v 10-11; Mathew 19 v 3-6.
A divorce that occurred when a person was an unbeliever can be pardoned.
2 Corinthians 7 v 39.
Remarriage
We believe that young widows are encouraged to remarry.
1 Corinthians 7 v 39
Homosexuality and Lesbianism
We believe that marriage is ordained by God for male and female (man and woman) only. Same sex marriage is forbidden.
Genesis 2 v 22-24; Romans 1 v 25-28.

Our Passions

Prayerfulness Righteousness Salvation of Souls, Studying the Word Fasting and praying Praising God through songs and dance Empowering people to righteously prosper in business and every other area of life through practicing God’s Word

Throne Room Church (TRC) was founded by the visionary, apostle, author and Senior Pastor Really Deve in May 2007 after heeding the call from God to start a church which was to be birthed at family level. TRC celebrates its 15th anniversary on 1st May 2022 after birthing five TRC Assemblies, three in Mashonaland Central province, Harare, Zimbabwe and two in Manicaland province Zimbabwe. TRC currently has a membership of more than 350.TRC as a church in its growth path has of essence adopted the Episcopal type of church governance or administration which is most effective in crystalizing and solidifying systems for efficient operations and expansion of God’s Kingdom work through following guidance from the visionary and apostle. The voice of the visionary must be heard as she hears from God and implement in obedience to God’s call.

TRC LEADERSHIP

Jesus Christ commissioned the 12 disciples after thoroughly training them to lead by example in preaching the truth of the gospel and in their day to day life style living. TRC has a carefully selected strong team of leaders that are Holy and Spirit filled and have under gone training in ministering the truth of the gospel and are also proficient in other areas as the work of God demands.
Mathew 28 v 19-20

OUR CAUSES

TRC is a bible reading and bible teaching church with a very profound powerful teaching anointing and very strong praise and worship team which when in praise through songs, brings down the glory of God as people witness and experience deliverances of all kinds during such times. Our vision extends to reach into the present, the immediate, the long term future both in the physical and eternal with the truth of the gospel of Jesus Christ thereby empowering people through righteous living which qualifies us for God’s blessings here on earth and in heavenly eternity.

Main Objective

The main purpose of establishing TRC is, by the grace of God, and in obedience to the call of God, to act as a Christian not-for-profit charitable and ecclesiastical institution of public character in Zimbabwe and the rest of the world, to go and teach the Truth of the Word of God to all peoples, to minister healing and deliverance from the powers of darkness to all peoples of the nations. Mathew 28 v 19-20

Consistent with the main objective, it is further the objective of TRC to fulfil its calling in line with the Founder’s vision, which is among others:
To carry out the Great Commission of our Lord Jesus Christ by being an Excellent network that will facilitate the building of relationships for the purpose of equipping, encouraging and motivating one another, by spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all parts of the world through every available conceivable means and to teach the truth to all peoples of all the nations based on the Word of God.
